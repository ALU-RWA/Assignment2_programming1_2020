Question 2 :

ALU Hangman

The program asks you 10 questions about ALU.
Every time you miss a question (you are hanging the man )
If you miss six question (the man dies ) And the game stops
If you get all the questions right , your man lives , you get congratulated and the game stops
If you finish all the questions with less than 6 mistakes , your man lives , and you are told the number of mistakes you made , and the game stops .

  1. When was ALU founded ? (2015)
  2. Who is the CEO of ALU ? (Fred Swaniker)
  3. Where are ALU campuses ? (Rwanda and Mauritius)
  4. How many campuses does ALU have? (2)
  5. What is the name of ALU Rwanda's Dean? (Gaidi Faraj)
  6. Who is in Charge of Student Life? (Sila Ogidi)
  7. What is the name of our Lab? (Nigeria)
  8. How many students do we have in year 2 Cs? (put the number of students)
  9. How many degrees does ALU offer? (8)
  10. Where are the headquarters of ALU? (Mauritius)

Grading Rubric

Hanging the man at the right time 20 marks
Ending the game at the right time 15 marks
Well handling user input 10 marks (eg: Fred Swaniker , FRed Swaniker , fred swaniker and FRED SWANIKER are the right answer ,
meaning you ignore the case (upper or lower) while dealing with the strings here )
Code well commented 5 marks
